How It Works

Step 1

Want to plan your next adventure? Enter a city you want to travel to in the search bar above.

Step 2

Discover postcards posted by other travelers, like yourself. Feel free to post your own as well!

Step 3

Add postcards to your collection of items. Or, view collections that other travelers have previously created.

Step 4

Export a collection to a printable document, that way you can take your to-do bucket list on your next adventure.
